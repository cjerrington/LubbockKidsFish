About Kid Fish Lubbock

Kidfish Lubbock is a free event for you and your family and will be Saturday, April 20th, 2019, 7 a.m. – 1 p.m. at Maxey Park. Free food will be provided with registration. The lake will be stocked with channel catfish ready for your family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do we need a fishing license?

Texas Parks & Wildlife Department waives the fishing license requirement for adults to fish with their kids ONLY at this event from 7AM-1PM. Kids always fish free up 17 years of age

Will I need to bring bait?

Bait is provided by Cabela's

Do I need to bring a fishing pole?

If you have one, great! Bring it to try it out, but if not, poles will be available to borrow at registration. Quantity limited.

Is registration neccessary?

All must either pre-register or register at event. Pre-registered participants still have to check in upon arrival to receive event wristband for lunch and other freebies including free door prizes!

Will we get anything from the event?

First 500 kids to the event get free t-shirts and all kids will get a small goodie bag.

Can I keep what we catch?

Maxey will be stocked with Channel Catfish which in community ponds there are no minimum size requirements to keep what you catch but can only keep up to 5.

All other fish caught must adhere to size requirements and bag limits set by Texas Parks & Wildlife. See Outdoor Annual app or Texas Parks and Wildlife website.

Lunch? Really??

Lunch will be provided at 11 AM.

Is there anything else I should bring?

Bring a chair or blanket, sunscreen and smile!
